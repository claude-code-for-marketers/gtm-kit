# Persona: Head of Growth / Performance Marketing

*Primary champion and, at most ICP accounts, the economic buyer. Status: pre-launch — validate with design partners.*

---

## Overview

**Title(s):** Head of Growth, Director/VP of Performance Marketing, Head of Paid Media, Growth Marketing Lead
**Typical seniority:** Manager → VP
**Decision role:** Champion + economic buyer (at 20–500-person companies they often own the tool budget)
**Found at companies:** Funded DTC/ecommerce, B2B SaaS, fintech, and consumer-app brands, 20–500 employees

---

## What They Care About

**Their primary metric:** Efficient customer acquisition — CAC, blended ROAS, pipeline/signups per dollar across channels.

**Their biggest problem right now:** Saturated paid social/search with rising CAC. They're under pressure
to find new channels that work — but newsletter advertising feels opaque. There's no "ad library" to see
where competitors spend, so testing it feels like guessing.

**What a good week looks like:** Found a new channel or placement that beats their blended CAC, with data
to defend the spend to their founder/CFO.

**What a bad week looks like:** Burned budget on a placement that didn't convert, with no way to have known
in advance — and a competitor quietly scaling a channel they can't see.

---

## How They Buy

**Involvement in purchase:** Initiates and approves (small team) / strongly influences (larger team)
**Discovery behavior:** Peers in growth Slack/Discord communities, marketing newsletters, Twitter/LinkedIn, "how we grew" content
**Evaluation style:** Data-driven and fast — wants to see the product do something useful immediately (the no-signup sample lookup does this before any commitment)
**Common objections:**
- "Can't I just check Who Sponsors Stuff?" → "That's a historical archive built for newsletters to find advertisers. We show *you* where competitors run right now and where to spend next."
- "Is your coverage complete?" → "We cover what matters in your vertical; if a newsletter you care about is missing, we add it. No tool has 100% — we're the most useful for buyers."
- "I don't have budget for another tool" → "Run the free sample lookup and see your competitor's footprint first. If you subscribe, there's a 14-day money-back guarantee — one placement it saves you pays for the year."

---

## How to Reach Them

**Best channel:** Email + LinkedIn; reinforced by presence in growth communities and marketing newsletters
**Best time:** Tuesday-Thursday business hours; trigger-based outreach should go out within 48 hours of a relevant signal.
**What gets their attention:** A *specific* competitor footprint — "here's every newsletter [their competitor] ran last quarter." Concrete, about them, not generic.
**What gets ignored:** Generic "newsletter ads are booming" pitches; anything that sounds like a prospecting/lead-gen tool

---

## Message Framework

**Value prop for this persona:**
See exactly where your competitors advertise in newsletters — and get a ranked plan of where you should spend next.

**Proof points that resonate before launch:**
- A real competitor footprint pulled live during the conversation
- Freshness: "new sponsors detected this week in your category"

**Reference customers for this persona:** None yet — use live product evidence until design partners are referenceable.

---

## Sample Outreach Hook

> "[Competitor] is running in 7 newsletters your buyers read — most growth teams have no way to see that.
> Want their full footprint and the 3 placements they own that you're missing?"
